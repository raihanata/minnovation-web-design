AlphaX Data Platform™

Edge to Insight

Transform data from sensors and field devices into insights for your team.

Connect and collaborate from asingle pane of glass.

With AlphaX™ you can seamlessly connect your data from LPWAN, fieldbus and other time-series sources with the decision makers in your teams. You can easily create and share mind-blowing visualisations and put the right information into the hands of the right people at the right time. AlphaX will help you accelerate the work of your data scientists and business analysts… and it will do all of this from a single pane of glass.
